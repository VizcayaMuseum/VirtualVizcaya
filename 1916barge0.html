Examine the 1916 Barge

This digital recreation depicts the Barge as it might have looked when it was completed in 1916. Using photographs and drawings from Vizcaya’s archives, we created this historic model to show the original detail and surface texture that have been lost over 100 years.

This model also offers our conservation professionals clues about the continued loss that may occur into the future so we can plan accordingly.
