What Is 3D Documentation?

3D documentation refers to the process of gathering precise measurements and visual data of objects and spaces. This data can be used to create highly detailed images, 3D models and prints, and virtual experiences. Ongoing 3D documentation also enables us to record erosion and loss over time, helping us make informed conservation decisions.
